About the Game

"Who is the Imposter?" is a party game where players are divided into Crewmates and Imposters. The Crewmates must work together to find out who the Imposters are, while the Imposters will try to blend in and deceive the others.

The Crewmates are given a secret word, while the Imposters only have a hint. Everyone will take turns asking each other questions, and at the end of each round, players will vote to kick out the player they think is the Imposter.

The game continues until all Imposters are found or until the Imposters eliminate all Crewmates. The challenge is to determine who can deceive the others the best!
